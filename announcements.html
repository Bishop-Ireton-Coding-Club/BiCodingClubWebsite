Coding Club

    Coding Club has their Interest Meetings!
  • Yes, you read that right: Meetings. Coding club wanted everyone to get a chance to get an in- person feel for the club so they hosted two seperate meetings. Of course, remote students could also join on the second day as well. Coding Club had the A-K members come on Wednesday, September 9 and the L-Z people, remote people, and anyone who wanted to join but couldn't make it on Wednesday joined Coding Club on Friday, September 11 after school in room 313. Coding club had a great turnout and is super excited to kick the year off. For the first week, Coding Club wants to work on and teach Python! Time to learn how to code :)

    • Coding Club Gets Ready to Welcome Its New Members! (9/7/2020)
  • Coding Club's leaders discuss how to prepare everyone and get ready for the interest meeting. As of now, Coding Club has a little under 40 interested students! This is amazing for Coding Club to see so much interest among under and upperclassmen alike. Don't forget, the interest meeting for A-K people will be on Wednesday September 9 and on Friday September 11 for the L-Z group. The leaders are super excited to welcome everyone to the club and for a great year!

    • Weekly Summer Meetings Continue as Coding Club Gets Ready for School (8/26/2020)
  • Coding Club has meetings every Wednesday as the leaders are gearing up for the school year. Coding Club has about 21 members as of now, but with the club fair coming up in a week Coding Club is ready to welcome new members! We encourage everyone to join Coding Club, even if you may not think you know much about computers because we are here to help anyone from beginner level to an experienced coder.

    • Coding Club Takes on a Website! (8/13/2020)
  • Coding Club leaders have begun to code a website using HTML, CSS, and Javascript for anyone in any Computer Science related club or society. The website is meant to be a place for all Computer Science students at Bishop Ireton to come together and stay current on all clubs and CSHS. The website is still in the process of being coded, but it is expected to look good in the fall!

    • Projects Begin! (07/24/2020)
  • Coding Club has begun projects! Members and leaders have begun working on projects both in teams or individually. Projects are meant to be creative and can be anything at all using any language or software. Stay up to date on projects and Coding Club here.

    • Coding Club Weekly Meetings (6/5/2020)
  • During the summer, Coding Club has been having weekly meetings every Wednesday at 2pm. If you're interested, contact one of the leaders (Contacts page) and you'll be in! In coding club you will create projects, learn new programming languages, and be exposed to Computer Science topics.
